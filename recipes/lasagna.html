Lasagna

Description

This is a really famous Italian recipe, originated in the city of Naples.

It is commonly known as Garfield favorite dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chunk
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced
  • 2 gloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of good-quality tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces of tomato paste
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons of dried oregano
  • 1/ cup of chopped fresh parsley, packed
  • 1 tablespoon of Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch of garlic powder and7or garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon of red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup of sugar
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Put pasta water on to boil

  2. Brown the ground beef

  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef

  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce

  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles

  6. Heat the oven to 190ÂºC

  7. Assemble the lasagna

  8. Bake

  9. Cool and serve
